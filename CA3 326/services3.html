Personal Styling Services

  • Virtual Styling Consultations: Many brands, including Ormate, offer virtual styling consultations where customers can connect with a stylist to get personalized outfit recommendations.
  • In-Store Styling: Brands often have stylists in-store who can help customers find outfits that match their body type, preferences, and lifestyle.
  • Online Style Guides and Lookbooks: Seasonal style guides, lookbooks, and trend reports provide inspiration, helping customers visualize how to wear different items.

Custom Fit and Tailoring

  • Custom Sizing: Some brands allow customers to order clothing in custom sizes to ensure a perfect fit.Custom Fit and Tailoring
  • Tailoring and Alterations: Brands may offer complimentary or discounted tailoring to adjust garments to fit individual customers, often available in flagship stores.

Delivery and Return Services

  • Free or Expedited Shipping: Many brands offer fast and free shipping, especially for loyalty members or on orders above a certain amount.
  • Return and Exchange Policies: Brands with customer-centric policies allow easy returns or exchanges to improve the shopping experience.
  • In-Store Pickup: Customers can often pick up online orders in-store, which can reduce shipping costs and allow for faster access to purchases

Aftercare Services for Clothing

  • Repair Services: High-end or sustainable brands often offer repair services for damaged clothing items to extend the product’s life.
  • Cleaning and Maintenance Tips: Brands may provide guides on how to care for their products properly to maintain quality and durability, especially for delicate materials.
  • Recycling and Repurposing Programs: For customers ready to part with items, some brands offer recycling programs where old clothing can be returned for responsible disposal or repurposing.

Customer Support and Care Services

  • 24/7 Customer Support: Some brands provide around-the-clock customer service through live chat, phone, or email for assistance with orders, sizing, or product inquiries.
  • Personalized Recommendations: Customer support teams often help with tailored product recommendations based on customers’ needs and past purchases.
